# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
This project is dedicated to providing **open, accessible, and empowering financial tools** for low-income individuals. We are committed to fostering a **safe, respectful, and inclusive community** for all contributors, users, and participants, regardless of age, background, experience, or identity.

We pledge to act with integrity, professionalism, and kindness, ensuring that everyone can participate without fear of harassment or discrimination.

## Expected Behavior
Contributors and community members are expected to:
- Use **inclusive and respectful language** in all communications.
- Treat differing opinions and experiences with **empathy and respect**.
- Focus on solutions and constructive feedback, not personal attacks.
- Promote **financial literacy, transparency, and empowerment** through their contributions.
- Follow best practices in coding, documentation, and version control.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.
- Public shaming or personal attacks.
- Intentionally disrupting discussions or contribution workflows.
- Using the project to promote unrelated agendas or exploit users.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both **within the repository and any public spaces** where participants represent the project.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers will take appropriate action, which may include:
- Warnings or guidance
- Temporary suspension of contributions
- Permanent removal from the project

Our goal is to maintain a **community that is safe, respectful, and supportive** for all participants.
